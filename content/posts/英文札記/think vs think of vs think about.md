## think

- 表示「認為、覺得、考慮」或單純的思考動作
- 使用情境：表達意見、信念、推測

**例句**

- I think it will rain today.
- I think we should arrive at the restaurant before 5 P.M. tomorrow.

---

## think of

- 提出一個想法；回想；腦中浮現某人/某事
- 使用情境：想到某人、某事、某點子

**例句**

- When I think of Taiwan, I think of night markets. </br>
- When I tasted the sashimi[^1] at the restaurant, I thought of Japan and wanted to plan a trip there.

[^1]: 刺身（さしみ）, 生魚片

---

## think about

- 花時間仔細思考、考慮且帶有持續性
- 使用情境：深入思考、權衡、計畫

**例句**

- I’m thinking about changing jobs. </br>
- I'm thinking about going abroad and living there for a long time. But I haven't made any plans yet.

---

## 總結

- **think** →「表達意見」→「我覺得」
- **think of** →「瞬間念頭」→「我突然想到」
- **think about** →「持續思考」→「我正在考慮仔細思考」
